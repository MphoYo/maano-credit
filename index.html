Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to apply?

You will need your valid South African ID, your latest payslip, and your last 3 months bank statements. Additional documents may be required depending on the assessment.

Who can apply for a loan?

You must be 18 or older, earn a regular income, have a valid South African ID, and be able to provide proof of income and bank statements.

How long does approval take?

During business hours, many applications are processed on the same day, provided we receive all required documents.

Will you do a credit check?

Yes. As a responsible lender we must perform affordability and credit checks. An enquiry does not automatically mean a default or judgment.

How do I repay the loan?

Repayments are usually collected via debit order on your agreed pay date. Details will be in your loan agreement.

What if I struggle to pay?

Contact us immediately so we can discuss possible arrangements. Ignoring the problem can lead to extra costs and legal action.
